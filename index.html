TAIPING BAMBOO RESORT

Our resort is located on lush hills near Taiping city, surrounded by authentic rain forest and mountain streems. We provide different types of accomodation and can host up to 50 peole. We have a camping site, open kitchen, natural swimming pond and two open halls for group acivities.

ACCOMODATION TOUR SERVICE
  1. Single Storey Bamboo Lodge - this bamboo lodge has an attached toilet with hot shower, Air Con, fan and veranda with bamboo furniture. RM 160, and RM 230 on weekends. (Under construction at the moment.)
  2. Double Storey Bamboo Lodge - this bamboo lodge has two levels. There is a veranda and toilet with hot shower at the first level. Sleeping room with bamboo furniture (a wardrobe, dressing table with mirror, chairs and coffee table), Air Con, fan and balcony are on the second level. There is a queen size bed in the sleeping room and two floor mattresses can be provided for extra RM 30 each (this price includes breakfast). Suitable for a family group up to 4 people. RM 210, and RM 330 on weekends.
  3. Deluxe Fiber Lodge - this type of room has TV, Air Con, fan and an attached toilet with a hot shower. Two floor mattresses are available for extra RM 30 each (this price incluedes breakfast). Suitable for a family group up to 4 people. RM 160, and RM 230 on weekends.
  4. Two Rooms Family Suite - this family suite has two rooms connected by inside door. Each room has an attached toilet with a hot shower, TV, Air Con, fan, a king size bed and two floor mattresses can be provided for free.  RM 350, and RM 550 on weekends. The price doesn't include breakfast. Suitable for family groops up to 8 people.
  5. Three Rooms Family Suite - this family suite has two rooms connected by inside door. Each room has an attached toilet with a hot shower, TV, Air Con, fan, a king size bed and two floor mattresses can be provided for free. RM 550, and RM 750 on weekends. The price doesn't include breakfast. Suitable for family groops up to 12 people.
